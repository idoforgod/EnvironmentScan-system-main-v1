### 8.2 FSSF Classification Methodology

The FSSF (Future Signal Scanning Framework) 8-type classification system categorizes signals based on their position in the futures landscape:

- **Weak Signal**: Low visibility, potentially high impact if amplified. Requires pattern confirmation.

- **Discontinuity**: Breaks from established patterns. Represents paradigm shifts.
- **Driver**: Active forces causing change. Requires strategic response.
- **Emerging Issue**: Growing visibility, developing impact. Requires monitoring escalation.
- **Precursor Event**: Concrete events that trigger system-level transitions.
- **Trend**: Established directional patterns. Requires adaptation strategies.
- **Megatrend**: Multi-decade structural forces. Requires fundamental strategic alignment.

Priority mapping: CRITICAL (Weak Signal, Wild Card, Discontinuity), HIGH (Driver, Emerging Issue, Precursor Event), MEDIUM (Trend, Megatrend).
