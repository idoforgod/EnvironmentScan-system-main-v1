### 8.2 FSSF Classification Methodology

The Future Signal Scanning Framework (FSSF) classifies signals into 8 types based on their probability, impact, and temporal characteristics:

- **Weak Signal**: Low probability, potentially high impact, early-stage indicators of future change. Require sustained monitoring.
- **Wild Card**: Very low probability events that have materialized with very high impact. The Iran supreme leader death is a textbook Wild Card.
- **Discontinuity**: Breaks in established patterns that indicate structural change. None detected in this scan beyond the geopolitical crisis.

- **Precursor Event**: Events that signal potential larger changes ahead. SpaceX listing and China-US confrontation are Precursor Events.
- **Trend**: Established directional movements with clear evidence. KOSPI rally and semiconductor exports are current Trends.
- **Megatrend**: Large-scale, slow-moving forces that shape societies over decades. Science education engagement represents a Megatrend.
