### 8.2 FSSF Classification Methodology

- **Weak Signal**: Low visibility, early-stage indicators of potential change. Require active monitoring.

- **Emerging Issue**: Growing visibility and momentum but not yet mainstream. Key transition category.
- **Precursor Event**: Early warnings of larger systemic changes. Require root cause analysis.
- **Trend**: Established directions with clear momentum and evidence base.
- **Megatrend**: Large-scale, long-duration forces that shape entire systems. Largely irreversible.

Classification is based on signal maturity (evidence base depth), uncertainty (outcome variability), temporal scope (H1/H2/H3), and system impact (breadth and depth of affected domains).
