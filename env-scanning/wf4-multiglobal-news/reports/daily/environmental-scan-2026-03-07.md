### 8.4 FSSF Classification Methodology

FSSF (Future Signal Scanning Framework) 8-type classification system, applied by LLM (@phase2-analyst) in Phase 2:

- **Weak Signal**: Low amplitude, early-horizon evidence of structural change. Characterized by sparse sourcing, expert disagreement, and H3 time horizon.
- **Emerging Issue**: Growing but not dominant pattern. Multiple independent sources, increasing volume, H2-H3 horizon.
- **Trend**: Sustained directional movement with clear momentum. Multi-source confirmation, H1-H2 horizon.
- **Megatrend**: Long-duration structural force at civilization scale. Rarely detected in 24h windows.
- **Driver**: Long-term force multiplier that amplifies other signals. Enables or constrains multiple STEEPs domains.
- **Wild Card**: High-impact, low-probability, discontinuous event. Classified by impact score 4-5 + uncertainty High/Radical + unexpected emergence.
- **Discontinuity**: Structural break from prior trajectory. Point of no return in a system. High impact, confirmed break from baseline.
- **Precursor Event**: Observable early-warning signal for a larger anticipated event. Leading indicator function.
